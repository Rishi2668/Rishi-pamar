Admission Process:

                    WEI admits students based on their performance in the assessment test. It is important for parents and prospective applicants 
                    to understand the selection policy.
                    A written assessment and interview will be conducted. Parents will be notified about the date for the written assessment and interview.
                    The written assessment will be conducted as follows
                    1. Grade I to Grade III: General Knowledge
                    2. Grade IV & Grade V: English & Mathematics.
                    3. Grade VI to Grade IX : English, Mathematics and Logical Reasoning.
                    4. Grade XI : English, Mathematics, Science and Logical Reasoning.
                    Examinaton will be of 100 marks in the mcq model.Each question carries one mark.
                    Fees concession will be given based on obtained marks.
